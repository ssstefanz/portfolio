BWFH: Baking & Working From Home

helping you rise from your desk

What's this site for?

You! A person sitting on your computer all day who has a body and needs it to stand up and do something every so often. The schedules run on pagnotta timers (basically a pomodoro) that'll get you out of your seat and touching dough for a couple minutes. If all goes well, around dinner time, you should have fresh bread.

But I don't know anything about baking.

Not a problem! In the menu below, you can navigate to pages for different levels of bakers: beginner, intermediate, advanced. Within each you can adjust recipes, futzing with hydration, flour types, and so on.
